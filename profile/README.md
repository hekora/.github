A modern remake and reimagining of the **original Transformice Adventures**.

Re: TFMA aims to bring back the charm, humor and cooperative spirit of the original TFMA with:

- Story-driven quests  
- Dungeons with unique mechanics  
- Co-op gameplay  
- Combat, spells and boss encounters  
- Inventory, items, crafting and progression  
- A lively world inspired by the classic TFM universe  

This is a community-driven revival project made with respect for the original game‚Äôs design philosophy.

---

### **Below The Line**  
A cooperative ocean-survival adventure where two players rely on each other to survive:

- One player manages the boat above the waves  
- The other dives underwater with an oxygen tether  
- Collect materials, fight pressure hazards and upgrade your vessel  
- Trade with roaming ships and discover hidden islands  
- Explore the vast unknown beneath the surface
